This website was created to help new and incoming software developers and computer science students. Software development is such a large field of study many new learners are daunted by how much they need to know. Our website is here to serve as a library of information and various resources. These resources consist of various learning mediums such as links to articles, videos, live tutorials, and more.

This is a project created and managed by Elita Danilyuk for CS312 as an honors option at Colorado State University.
