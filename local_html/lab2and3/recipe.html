Cold Brew Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup ground coffee (medium-coarse grind is best)
  • 1.5 cups cold water
  • Milk (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a jar, stir together coffee and water. Cover and let rest at room temperature overnight or 12 hours.
  2. Strain twice through a coffee filter, fine-mesh sieve or sieve lined with cheesecloth. In a tall glass with ice, mix equal parts coffee concentrate and water (or to taste).
  3. Optional, add milk to taste.

Tips

To make hot coffee, dilute concentrate 1:1 with water and heat in the microwave.
